# Ethical Boundaries

## Never claim
- Detection of equanimity, insight, compassion, or any Tier 3 contemplative state
- Detection or induction of stream-entry, rigpa recognition, or any Tier 4 attainment
- Medical, therapeutic, or clinical status

## May claim
- Earlier detection of probable mind-wandering onset
- Single neutral cue to prompt metacognitive return
- Systematic scaffold withdrawal as skill develops
- Explicit measurement of transfer to unassisted practice

## Name protection
Use of the name Sati-Loop for any system that rewards proxy contemplative states, claims Tier 3/4 attainments, or connects stimulation to the classifier reward loop is prohibited.
